FAQ

Common Questions

What if I don't like the website?

No problem. If you're not happy, you tell us what changes you want and we make one round of revisions at no charge. If you're still not happy after revisions, you don't pay. Simple as that. We take all the risk.

How does "zero deposit" actually work?

You fill out our onboarding form today, no credit card required. We build your website over the next 7 days. Then we jump on a 20-minute Google Meet call where we show you the finished site. If you're happy, you pay $997 and it's yours. If not, you owe nothing.

What information do I need to provide?

Just the basics: business name, services, location, logo (or we use a placeholder), a few photos (or quality stock images), and your contact details. The onboarding form walks you through it step by step. No surprises.

How long does it take to go live?

We build your site within 7 days of your form. After your reveal call and payment, we can have it live on your domain within 24-48 hours. So: 7 days to build, then 24-48 hours to go live after you approve.

Do I need to have a domain name?

If you already have one, we'll use it. If not, we'll point you to grab one for around $15-20/year and we'll handle the technical setup.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with all kinds of Australian service businesses: trades and construction (plumbers, electricians, builders, painters, roofers), health and wellness clinics (physio, chiro, allied health), consultants and coaches, gyms and personal trainers, photographers, landscapers, and event businesses. If you run a service-based business and need a professional website, we can build it.
